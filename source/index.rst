Welcome to HomeGallery's documentation!
=======================================

Discover your memories
----------------------

Explore your photos and videos safe from home with an open mind and free from cloud and vendors.
HomeGallery is a mobile friendly web gallery to serve all your private media.

Please visit the `demo HomeGallery <https://demo.home-gallery.org/>`_ and start exploring my public photos.

.. note::
   The current state of this project is in early alpha status. This pet project was started to solves my
   needs and requires CLI/terminal interactions.
   The most tested platform is Linux/Debian.
   It runs also on Mac or Windows but are barley tested.
